3. Definitions

  • Personal data: any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • Processing: any operation performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means.
  • Data Controller: the legal entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.
  • Data Processor: the natural or legal person that processes personal data on behalf of the Data Controller.
  • Data Subject: the natural person to whom the personal data relates.
  • Consent: any freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of the Data Subject's wishes.

10. Data Security

We adopt appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption in transit: all communications via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: data on Firebase/Google Cloud encrypted with AES-256
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with hashed passwords (bcrypt/scrypt)
  • App Check: verification that requests originate from the genuine Fink Personal app (Play Integrity / App Attest)
  • Access control: each user can only access their own data (owner-scoped)
  • Rate limiting: protection against brute-force and DDoS attacks
  • Monitoring: crash reporting and anomaly detection (Sentry)
  • Principle of least privilege: each component only has access to the data strictly necessary
