3. Description of the Service

Fink Personal is a personal finance application that offers:

  • Income and expense tracking
  • Automatic tax calculation (IRPEF, IMU, capital gains, social security contributions) for Italy, France, Germany, and Spain
  • Customized tax deadline calendar with exact dates and regulatory references
  • Medium to long-term financial forecasting
  • AI assistant for tax and financial questions
  • Receipt scanner for automatic expense recording
  • Customized tax reports

The calculation engine uses deterministic mathematical formulas based on current tax legislation. Artificial intelligence is used exclusively for the chat assistant and receipt scanner, not for tax calculations.
